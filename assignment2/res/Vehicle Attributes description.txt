CarID: Unique 9-digit identifier for each vehicle.
VehicleType: The type of the vehicle (e.g., Sedan, SUV, Hatchback, Pickup Truck, Hybrid).
Speed: Maximum speed (in km/h).
Fuel: Fuel tank capacity (in liters).
Seats: Number of seats.
Year: Year of manufacture.
CargoCapacity: Cargo capacity (only for Pickup Truck), in kilograms.
ElectricRange: Range on electric power only (only for Hybrid vehicles), in kilometers.
Drivetrain: Type of drivetrain (AWD, FWD, 4WD, etc.).
Price: Price of the vehicle (in dollars).
